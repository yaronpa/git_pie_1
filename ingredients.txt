Dough for single-crust pie
1.2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/3 teaspoon salt
2 cups water
3 large egg yolks, beaten, room temperature
1 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
MERINGUE:
3 large egg whites, room temperature
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sugar
